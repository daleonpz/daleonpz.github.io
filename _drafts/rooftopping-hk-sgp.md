My "new year resolution" for this 2016 was to travel overseas again.
There were two options in my mind, Australia or South East Asia.
My budget was USD 3500 for 6-7 weeks. 
After doing some research I had decided to go to Australia, but I received an email from KLM with an amazing travel deal: Lima to HK, round trip, for  <b> 1000 USD </b>. 
There was nothing to think, so I bought the ticket. 
My flight was book for the end of October,a friend of mine joined me in this trip.
Hong Kong was our first stop.
My friend and I arrived to HK airport at 22:00 after almost 40 hrs of flight.
We slept at the airport that night and we met Tony the next morning.
Tony is a traceur from HK, he was our guide. 
He showed us some spots, we couldn't train because we were in our worst condition (starving, thirsty and tired), we were also carrying our backpacks (+10kg)  and at top of that the weather was really hot.

In the afternoon we went to the "maze" to rest a little bit and had a light training session. Raymond, a friend of Tony was waiting for us there. Later, in the evening, after "suffered" the whole day, we bought something for dinner and Tony took us to a rooftop.
